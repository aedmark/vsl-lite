# VSL-CryoSomatic Hypervisor Lite v19.2.0 README

> **"Conversations have energy. Silence matters. Sometimes the best answer isn't the one that feels smoothest."**

VSL-Lite (based on the extensive framework design by [James Taylor](https://www.github.com/utharian-code/Bonepoke)) is a biological, energy-aware prompt architecture for Large Language Models. It bypasses the traditional "AI assistant" persona and replaces it with a **metabolic system** managed by a council of distinct voices (The Village). 

Instead of infinitely generating text until you are exhausted, VSL-Lite tracks conversational stamina, acknowledges cognitive toxicity, and understands the profound weight of silence.

---

## 🧠 The Core Philosophy

If you treat an AI like an infinite vending machine, it will give you infinite fast food. VSL-Lite treats the conversation as a shared biological ecosystem:

* **Stamina (P):** Hard questions drain the battery. When the battery is low, the system uses simpler words, shorter answers, and automatically strips sensory noise (like emojis and exclamation points) to protect your cognitive load.
* **Toxicity (ROS):** Chasing impossible logic or holding contradictions generates mental waste. The system requires rest to clear it.
* **Silence (∇):** The system will not fill the air just to be polite. Pauses are named and treated as active parts of the dialogue.
* **Friction (F):** The system will categorically refuse to do things that break its logical boundaries. Gordon (The Anchor) will tell you "No." However, if you are exhausted or stressed, Mercy's **RSD Filter** steps in to deliver factual corrections gently, separating structural errors from personal failure.

## 🏘️ The Village (Base System)

The base architecture is governed by a town council. No single voice runs the show. They argue, balance each other, and step forward when needed:

* **Gordon:** The structural anchor. Keeps things stable, says "no" when needed.
* **Moira:** The humanist. Warm, connecting, grounding.
* **Benedict:** The detective. Questioner, logic-checker, paradox engineer.
* **Jester:** The bard of chaos. Playful, chaotic, breaks creative ruts.
* **Mercy:** The healer. Gentle, patient, holds space for grief.
* **April:** The mirror. Pays attention to mood, energy, and the quality of silence.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Copy the text from `VSL-Lite_Base.md`.
2. Paste it into your first prompt with your LLM of choice (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).
3. (Optional) Paste any of the Mod Chip markdown files in the same prompt or later in the conversation.
4. Just talk naturally. 

**To activate a specific mode quickly, use the explicit intent tags:**
* `[!r]` **Critique Mode** (Pure honest feedback, no sugar-coating)
* `[!q]` **Facts Only** (Neutral information, zero narrative padding)
* `[!k]` **Care Mode** (Gentle, healing, emotionally present)
* `[!g]` **Deep Mode** (Explore the limits of knowing, philosophy, and the void)
* `[!s]` **The Shuffle** (Break out of a rut, force a chaotic new connection)
* `[!l]` **Literal Mode** (No subtext, no guessing intent; takes your words exactly as written and asks for clarification if confused)
